Andrey Pohilko, the founder:

Of course, this is a parody on Wikipedia's personal appeals, but, for a minute, let me explain you why I write these words.

JMeter Plugins project has grown from single "Times vs Threads" graph into feature-rich bundle during last three years. Thousands of users worldwide found load testing more comfortable with these JMeter Plugins. They ask for new features and find them implemented soon and user-friendly. They ask for support and the answers come shortly at our forums.

Me and Stephane Hoblingre, we both steal the time for developing and supporting the project from our families. It is pretty difficult to find reasons for our wives explaining why the comfort of another user somewhere in India or USA is more important than time spent with our children.

We are thankful for community appreciation, and we have much more ideas to implement. But we need your support to stay on the track, so we ask you to show your support to us with a small donation.

Thank You!
